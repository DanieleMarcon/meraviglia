# Meraviglia OS — Product Strategy

## Purpose
Define how Meraviglia OS evolves from a consultant operating environment into a strategic intelligence platform while preserving architectural discipline and tenant isolation.

## Conceptual Architecture
Product strategy is organized across three compounding capability tracks:

1. **Operational Core Track**
   - Stabilize intake, workspace orchestration, blueprint authoring, and governance workflows.
   - Ensure deterministic lifecycle transitions and auditable strategic decisions.

2. **Intelligence Track**
   - Expand simulation depth, indicator feedback loops, and evidence-based strategy adjustments.
   - Build reusable strategic assets via knowledge templates and modeling standards.

3. **Platformization Track**
   - Harden multi-tenant controls, RBAC, and interoperability contracts.
   - Enable modular extensions without coupling domain logic to infrastructure vendors.

## Core Strategic Principles
- **Lifecycle-first delivery**: prioritize end-to-end integrity of intake → workspace → blueprint before adding peripheral features.
- **Consultant leverage**: optimize for teams that design and govern strategy across many initiatives.
- **Controlled extensibility**: expand through explicit interfaces, templates, and adapters rather than direct coupling.
- **Strategic explainability**: every recommendation and simulation result must be interpretable by human decision-makers.
- **Data responsibility**: intelligence features must respect tenant boundaries and role-scoped visibility.

## Strategic Capability Priorities
1. **Foundation Priority**
   - Domain model consistency, tenant isolation guarantees, and application-layer orchestration.

2. **Modeling Priority**
   - Blueprint quality, scenario comparison, assumption tracking, and indicator governance.

3. **Intelligence Priority**
   - Simulation rigor, feedback assimilation, and progressively smarter strategy support.

4. **Ecosystem Priority**
   - Integration interfaces for CRM/automation systems with Meraviglia retaining strategic control.

## Future Extensibility Considerations
- Move from static strategy templates toward adaptive template systems informed by outcomes.
- Introduce portfolio-level strategic control views for consultants managing multiple workspaces.
- Add policy-driven autonomous recommendations with mandatory human review checkpoints.
- Evolve into a strategic intelligence platform that unifies planning, simulation, and governance across domains.
